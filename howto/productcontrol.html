Add / Delete / Modify a Product

by Digital808.com - Award Winning Web Site Design/Development

This tutorial will help you to understand how you can add / delete / modify a product. We will add a product called THX1138, then modify it and finally delete it.

First go ahead and log into Interchange.  If you do not have the username and password for Admin then please let me know.

ADDING PRODUCTS:

  1. click on Items (you should now be in the main area where all of your products reside).
  2. Click on CREATE NEW ITEM and fill out all of the appropriate fields:
    • SKU
    • Short Description - This is a brief snippet of sentence to tell users what this product is (go ahead and type in: First Movie by Lucas)
    • Product Group - This is the group in which the product will sit in (go ahead and select any of the groups already present)
    • Product Category - This is the category a product will sit in (again select one that is already present)
    • Product Price -
    • Image - This is the main image that the product will have.  Make sure you upload the image to www/cart/images/items
    • Thumb - This is the thumb image that the product will have. Make sure this is uploaded to www/cart/images/thumb
    • Detailed Description - A detailed description of what the item will have on it.
    • Weight in Pounds - This is important since it will be responsible for the products shipping costs.
    • There is one more thing that we need to do in order to show that the product is available in the Inventory.
      • Click on top on Inventory and inside of ON HAND put in a very large number regardless of your actual inventory.  I would recommend putting in 9999. The reason behind this is simple; you don't want your customers to leave a web site if you don't have that item.  If anything do your best to obtain this product and thats that.
      • Make sure it says IN STOCK and click on OK
    • OK thats all we will fill out since these are the basics of what a product needs to show up.
    • CLICK ON OK and we are done creating the product! If you want go ahead and go into your store and either do a search for the item we just made THX1138 or go to the category / subcategory you selected and find it that way.

MODYFYING A PRODUCT:

Go ahead and find the product you just created.  You may do so by Doing a Search for it inside of Interchange Items area.  Once you find it go ahead and click on the item.

We will now make this product have three available colors.

  • Go ahead and click on OPTIONS.  
  • Next click on SIMPLE OPTIONS.
  • We will now type in the following:
  • for NAME type in COLORS
  • for Label also type in COLORS.  Whats the difference between Name and Label?  Simple the Name is the word(s) that will show up next to the drop down selection and the Label is the word that will show up in the email you receive of what the customer selects.
  • Now in the big box type in the following and make sure every selection is separated by hitting the enter key.
      • Red
      • Ocean Blue
      • Yellow
  • Finally click the Create Option button and you're done!
  • Now if you look at your item you will see that it has the drop down selection you just created.

MODYFYING A PRODUCT WITH DIFFERENT PRICES:

Some products you have might need a slightly different price scheme.  For example X Large might cost $2 more then the other sizes of a product line. Here is how its done

  • Within your product click on OPTIONS
  • Now select the MATRIX OPTIONS, ONE WIDGET
  • and here you will have to create your new pull down choises.  Lets presume that Small, Medium, Large costs $20 and X Large costs $22 dollars. The fields you will have to fill out are: VARIANT DESCRIPTION, PRICE, ON HAND and WEIGHT.
  • so for a small you would fill out the following:
    • VARIANT DESCRIPTION = SMALL
    • PRICE = 20.00
    • ON HAND = 55555 (some large number)
    • WEIGHT = 1.5 (something pounds)
  • click on OK and repeat this process for the different sizes.  When you get to X Large you can just make the price equal 22.00

DELETING A PRODUCT:

OK we are done learning how to add and modify a product.  Now lets make sure that we get rid of it from the site so that customer do not see it.

Make sure you're in the Items Area (click on ITEMS) and then make sure you see the ITEM LIST.

Find the item that you want to delete and check the box to the left of it.

Now on the bottom click on Delete Checked Items and you're done!
