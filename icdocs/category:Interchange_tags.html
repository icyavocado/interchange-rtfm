Interchange tags

Interchange functions are accessed via the tags provided by the Interchange Markup Language (also known as ICML, ITL, IML and various other weird acronyms).  The Interchange Markup Language is a superset of the older MiniVend Markup Language (MML).  Interchange was called MiniVend until it was renamed in July 2000.

Interchange Markup Language tags provide access to Interchange's facilities by performing various data lookup, display and modification operations.  If you can't find what you need among the many predefined tags, then you can use the UserTag facility to create custom Interchange tags that do exactly what you want.
