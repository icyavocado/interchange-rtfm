PREFIX-price

Summary

  • [PREFIX-price noformat]

Description

The price of the product identified by the current code.

The result will be currency-formatted unless the optional "noformat" parameter is specified.  See the [currency] tag.

If Interchange's pricing routines cannot determine the price (i.e. if it is not a valid product or on-the-fly item), then zero will be returned.  If the loop is a hash list then the price will be modified by its quantity or other applicable attributes (such as "size", in the Standard ecommerce demo).
