The awful promotional logo used at LinuxTag 2007

Rather than simply use the professionally designed Interchange logo and typeface published on this page, the LinuxTag 2007 organisers thought they'd have a go at creating their own graphics for the event.  The cringeworthy result, republished here, was printed onto t-shirts, leaflets, carrier bags and other throwaway items.  Let's hope for a better effort in 2008.

Author: Jure Kodzoman.

Last update: 16 April 2007.

Interchange Sans (TrueType)

The new "Interchange Sans" typeface in TTF format.  This typeface started life as "Bitstream Vera Sans".  Although the typeface has been heavily modified from the original, the relatively free Bitstream Vera licence still applies.  The Bitstream Vera file archive contains a "COPYRIGHT.TXT" file which explains the terms of use.

Author: Kevin Walsh.

Version: 2.7.

Last update: 11 October 2007.

 41,104 InterchangeSans.ttf
Bitstream Vera (TrueType)

The original "Bitstream Vera" typeface in TTF format (tarred and gzipped).  See the "COPYRIGHT.TXT" file in the archive.

Author: Bitstream.

Version: 1.10.
